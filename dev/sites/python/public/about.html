Our Mission

We aim to provide a robust, secure, and accessible computing environment in the TJ Computer Systems Lab. Our mission is to empower students, support teachers, and foster a culture of technical excellence.

Our Values

We believe in open-source philosophy, transparency, continuous learning, and building tools that make a real impact. Collaboration, responsibility, and curiosity are at the heart of our work.

Meet the Team

The sysadmin team includes a diverse group of students passionate about Linux, scripting, networking, and hardware. We mentor newcomers and maintain a living knowledge base to keep our systems—and each other—growing.
